1. Explain step by step evaluation of 3 * x / y - z + k, where x = 2, y = 3, z = 3, k = 1

    Solution: <br />

    $$
    \begin{aligned}
    &3 * x / y - z + k \\
    &= (3 * 2) / 3 - 3 + 1 \\
    &= (6 / 3) - 3 + 1 \\
    &= (2 - 3) + 1 \\
    &= -1 + 1 \\
    &= 0
    \end{aligned}
    $$
